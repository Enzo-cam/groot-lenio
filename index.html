Groot is a fictional character from the Marvel universe, best known for his appearance in the Guardians of the Galaxy comics.
Groot is a being of the Flora colossus species, hailing from Planet X. Although he may seem intimidating due to his large size and tree-like appearance, Groot is kind-hearted and extremely loyal to his friends.
Groot has the ability to regenerate and grow at an incredibly fast pace, allowing him to create structures with his own body, as well as recover from severe injuries. He also has the ability to absorb wood to strengthen himself and can control other plants.
Groot is famous for his iconic phrase "I am Groot", which, despite seeming limited, can express a wide range of emotions and meanings; his friend Rocket can understand and translate all the subtleties of this unique language. He has been a much-loved character for his nobility, bravery, and sense of humor..
